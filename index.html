About Me

I am a senior year undergraduate at the Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur (IITK), majoring in Electrical Engineering. I am working on developing machine learning techniques for learning disabilities under the supervision of Prof. Pierre Dillenbourg, Computer-Human Interaction in Learning and Instruction (CHILI) Lab, École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL). In the Fall 2019 semester, I pursued a semester exchange at EPFL in Computer Science. I also did a Summer@EPFL 2019 research internship there.

Previously, I contributed to the open source probabilistic programming library Turing.jl as part of the Julia Seasons of Contributions program. I have done projects in Robotics under Prof. Ketan Rajawat (IITK). I did interns at Auquan Inc. and Kritsnam Technologies. I have also done projects on Machine Learning at IITK. For more details, please have a look at my CV here.

I like discussions on philosophy. I love playing Table Tennis. Do contact me if you are up for a match or two ;)
